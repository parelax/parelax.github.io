Q. What is it?

Parelax will be the ultimate application for gamers who love to play local multiplayer games online with friends, make new buddies, and build awesome communities! With Parelax, you can connect with gamers from around the world and enjoy your favorite local multiplayer games, no matter where you are.

Our platform allows you to easily invite friends to join your game, or find new players to join your squad. You can chat with your fellow gamers, share tips and tricks, and even build your own communities. Parelax is more than just a gaming platform, it's a community where gamers can come together to connect, play, and have fun.

So whether you're a casual gamer or a hardcore competitor, Parelax has something for you. Join our community today and experience the best of local multiplayer gaming, online!

Q. How does it work?

Parelax is a standalone application that allows you to play local multiplayer games online with friends. It's easy to use, and you can start playing in just a few minutes. Simply download the Parelax app, create an account, and start playing!

Parelax will be available for Windows, Mac, and Linux. We're also working on a mobile version for Android and iOS, so you can play your favorite local multiplayer games on the go!

Q. Is this Parsec?

No, Parelax is not Parsec. Parelax is it's own thing, completely separate from Parsec. We're not affiliated with Parsec in any way. You will not need to use Parsec Soda for hosting, and will have lots of other features that Parsec doesn't have.
